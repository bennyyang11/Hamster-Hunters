## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start Game**: Open http://localhost:3000
2. **Begin Battle**: Click "START GAME"
3. **Movement**: Use WASD keys
4. **Look Around**: Move mouse
5. **Jump**: Press Space (hamster agility!)
6. **Shoot**: Left-click to fire
7. **Reload**: Press R key
8. **Sprint**: Hold Shift
